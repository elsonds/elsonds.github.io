BYJUS ON-BOARDING SCREEN DESIGN

DESIGN

The average app loses 77% of its users within the first 3 days after the install. This makes the app on-boarding step a crucial part of the user journey. You never get a second chance to make a first impression.

The on-boarding process is a perfect place to show the product's key features and its value. Well-designed on-boarding experience increases the likelihood of users’ returning to the app.

PROTOTYPE

I designed an on-boarding screen for the Byju’s the learning app. I used an animated storyboard to list important features and top user benefits.

Three key messages were to be delivered:

  • Byju’s has the best teachers in India
  • Byju’s provides personalised learning
  • The student gets a plethora of resources and tools to learn their lessons


I designed three screens for each of the messages and used animation to provide progressive transitions instead of abrupt screen flips. By using a combination of text and imagery, I was able to craft a compelling on-boarding flow.
